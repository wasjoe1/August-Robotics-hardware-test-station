Instructions

0. Plug in Inclinometer
1. Click on Inclinometer button (Ensure Inclinometer is already plugged in else repeat from step 0)
2. Observe readings (Configurations, Data, State, User Information)
3. NG/G displays whether the Inclinometer has valid readings
4. Click on the required Baud rate (Only if the current baud rate is wrong/ invalid)
5. Check new readings as needed
6. Plug out the Inclinometer USB from the microcontroller to disconnect
* Ensure that the Inclinometer is the only component plugged in
* Do not plug in other hardware components while testing
