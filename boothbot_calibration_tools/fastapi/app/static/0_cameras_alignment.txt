1. Place the RB 4 meters away from the GS
2. Click the DISABLE_SERVOS button and click the LASER_ON button
3. Aim the laser at the RB
4. Click the ENABLE_SERVOS button
5. Click the RUN button
6. Adjust the position of the large camera or the small camera until the cameras_offset in the detailed data is within ±0.0003(gs) ±0.0005(lionel)
7. Click the DONE button when finished
