1. Along the y-axis of GS, one RB is placed at a distance of 7 meters from the GS, and the other RB is placed at a distance of -7 meters from the GS
2. Level the GS
3. Turn on the  laser level, adjust the  laser level, and aim the laser at the center of the long camera
4. Adjust the height of the RB until the laser of the laser level is aimed at the center point on the green light of the RB
5. Open RB
6. Click the RUN button
7. Wait for the job to finish
8. Click the SAVE_DATA button
9. Click the DONE button when finished
