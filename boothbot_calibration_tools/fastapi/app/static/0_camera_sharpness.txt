1. Prepare two RBs, one is placed 5 meters away from the GS, the other is placed 50 meters away from the GS, turn on the RBs
2. Adjust the ratio (aperture diameter: maximum aperture diameter) of the long camera to 18:100 and the short camera to 1:3
3. Click the RUN button
4. The value of long_camera_data and short_camera_data should be in (55-60, 225-255, 180-255)
5. Tighten the camera aperture screw
6. Resize the camera's focal plane until the sharpness_score value is at its highest
7. Tighten the focal plane screws
8. Use silicone and other items to fix the size camera
9. Click the DONE button when finished
