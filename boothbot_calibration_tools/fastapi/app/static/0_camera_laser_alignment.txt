Note: We can set param in this calibration.
1. For example Type "L=30" in input box, means calibrate camera laser alignment base 30meters distance.

1. Paste A4 paper on the wall 50 meters away from GS
2. Click DISABLE_SERVOS button
3. Click LASER_ON button
4. Move the servos until the laser hits A4 paper
5. Click ENABLE_SERVOS button
6. Click RUN button
7. Adjust the position of the laser until the laser on the web page image is stable within the yellow aperture
8. Put a RB beside A4 paper
9. Click TRY_TRACK button
10. The GS starts tracking the RB, the tester should observe whether the laser hits the correct position of the RB
11. Click DONE button when finished
