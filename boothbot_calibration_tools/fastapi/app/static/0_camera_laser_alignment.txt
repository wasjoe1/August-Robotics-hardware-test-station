1. Paste A4 paper on the wall 50 meters away from GS
2. Click DISABLE_SERVOS button
3. Click LASER_ON button
4. Move the servos until the laser hits A4 paper
5. Click LASER_OFF button
6. Adjust the position of the laser until the laser on the web page image is stable within the yellow aperture
7. Put a RB beside A4 paper
8. Click TRY_TRACK button
9. The GS starts tracking the RB, the tester should observe whether the laser hits the correct position of the RB
10. Click DONE button when finished
